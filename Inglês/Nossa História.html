Our story



Mercuryo S&Q was created on May 8, 2021, when seven people came up with the idea of entering the technology business. The company's name was inspired by “Mercury”, a god of Roman mythology associated with speed, which explains the concept of our name (Speed and Quality).






Provide services that meet the needs of our customers, exceeding expectations and offering much more than just services. To be a leader in the world of technology; Be recognized for being a reliable and innovative company. Innovation; Consistency; Commitment to customer satisfaction; Diversity in technology; Creativity.
